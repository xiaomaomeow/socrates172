Welcome to Socrates

Socrates is dedicated to providing mental health services for students in need. Our app offers gamified journaling, proven treatment methods, and personalized responses powered by AI. We believe that mental health support should be accessible to everyone as well as engaging.

How Socrates Can Improve Your Mental Health

Consistent Engagement

Socrates utilizes gamification to incentivize regular mental health journaling, promoting consistency in self-reflection and emotional processing.

Decreased Anxiety & Depression

With AI-driven responses, Socrates offers tailored guidance and resources, adapting to individual needs for effective mental health management.

Accessible Support for Stress

Through the convenience of a smartphone, Socrates provides access to evidence-based mental health resources and treatment methods, empowering users to take control of their well-being anytime, anywhere.

Positive Reinforcement

Users can earn rewards, badges, and unlockable features, fostering a sense of achievement and motivation to continue their journey towards improved mental health with Socrates.

Survey on Gamification

Now, what is gamification?

Gamification is a modern trend that centers on incorporating game mechanics into non-game contexts.

Popular gamification methods include:

  • log-in rewards for consecutive daily usage of apps

  • badges upon completion of achievements or gaining points

  • challenges and quests which provide users with a guided path

From a conducted user survey and interview of 45 responses from college students in different majors regarding gamification features:

  • On-going streak: 76% feel more motivated

  • Prize for consecutive log-ins: 69% feel more motivated.

  • Chat customization: 67% feel is very important.

  • Top 5 most engaging gamification: Leaderboards, space customization, collectibles/badges, earning points, and challenges.
